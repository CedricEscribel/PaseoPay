Frequently Asked Questions

If you miss a collection day, please store your garbage properly until the next scheduled collection. Do not leave garbage outside your home between collection days as this violates village rules and may attract pests.

For bulky items (furniture, appliances) or construction waste, please contact the village administration at 0968-741-3553 to arrange for special collection. Additional fees may apply based on the volume and type of waste.

Unpaid garbage collection fees accumulate and incur late payment penalties. After 3 months of non-payment, this may affect your standing with the HOA and could result in the suspension of garbage collection services for your household.

For missed collections or other issues with garbage service, please contact the village administration office at support@paseopay.com or call 0968-741-3553 during business hours.
