Frequently Asked Questions

HOA dues cover various community services including security, maintenance of common areas, street lighting, landscaping, and operation of community facilities like the clubhouse and swimming pool.

HOA dues are due on the 10th of each month and are considered late after a 5-day grace period. A late fee of ₱50 will be applied to payments made after the 15th of each month.

If you believe there is an error in your HOA charges, please contact the HOA office at support@paseopay.com or visit the village administration office during business hours with your receipt and documentation.
